Welcome to Yet Another Civilization Game!
Its a turn-based game about building empires and fighting
other civilizations. After you close this message, a game
creation window will be shown. There you will be able to
configure everything you want:
-word map size, amount of oceans and continents
-choose your civilization you want to play
-choose number of AI or human opponents
Also instead of generating random map you can load prebuilt
maps from file. Each civilization has two traits which determine
its strength and good sides, you can read about that in civilization
description.
